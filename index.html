It’s the comeback many collectors have been craving: the original 1986 TAG Heuer Formula 1 Series 1 is back, re-envisioned with the influential fashion lifestyle brand Kith. This dynamic collaboration reawakens a classic, which was Kith founder Ronnie Fieg's first watch that sparked his lifelong passion for watch collecting.

Paying tribute to the spirit of the original, TAG Heuer strived to track down the mould of the original case. Now enhanced with upgraded materials such as a sapphire crystal glass and a rubber strap, the collection heralds a new era for the TAG Heuer ‘80s icon.
